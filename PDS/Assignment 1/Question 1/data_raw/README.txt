Given dataset is related to Frailty which is a physical weakness due to lack of health or strength. The dataset contains data of 10 female participants and five different attributes.


Description of dataset:
1. Height: Height of participants in inches
2. Weight: Weight of participants in pounds
3. Age: Age of participants in years
4. Grip Strength: The amount of static force that the hand can squeeze around a dynamometer which is in kilograms.
5. Frailty: indicates presence or absence of the frailty. Y for yes and N for no.


Data is provided in the Comma Separated Values(CSV) file.
